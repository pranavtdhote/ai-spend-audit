# Landing Page Copywriting Strategy

The landing page copy is engineered for high conversion, focusing on a clear, quantifiable value proposition.

## Variant A (The "Waste" Angle - Current Default)
**Target Emotion**: Loss Aversion

- **H1**: Stop Overpaying for AI Tools.
- **H2**: Startups waste 30% of their AI budget on overlapping subscriptions and unused seats. Audit your stack in 60 seconds and instantly find your financial leaks.
- **Primary CTA**: Start Free Audit
- **Secondary CTA**: See How It Works
- **Social Proof Bar**: "Used by engineering teams at [Logo], [Logo], [Logo]"

### Key Sections:
1. **The Problem Statement**: "You're paying for Copilot. Your engineers are using Cursor. You're bleeding cash."
2. **The Mechanism**: "Our deterministic rule engine analyzes your specific tool stack against industry benchmarks to find redundant licenses, plan mismatches, and volume discount opportunities."
3. **The Guarantee**: "No signup required. No credit card. Just pure, actionable financial intelligence."

## Variant B (The "Optimization" Angle)
**Target Emotion**: Operational Excellence / Scaling

- **H1**: Scale Your AI Stack, Not Your Burn Rate.
- **H2**: The definitive spend intelligence platform for engineering leaders. Centralize, analyze, and optimize your team's AI tooling overhead.
- **Primary CTA**: Analyze My Stack
- **Secondary CTA**: View Sample Report

---

## Microcopy Guidelines
- **Buttons**: Never use "Submit". Use action-oriented phrases: "Generate Report", "Find Savings", "Analyze Stack".
- **Trust Indicators**: Near every input form, include: "🔒 Your stack data is evaluated locally and never shared."
- **Empty States**: If no tools are selected, don't say "No tools". Say "Add your first tool to unlock optimization insights."
