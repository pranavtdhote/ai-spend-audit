# NeuralCost Unit Economics & ARR Modeling

NeuralCost is a "Calculator as a Lead Magnet" model. The software itself is free, acting as a highly viral top-of-funnel (TOFU) acquisition channel for a premium Cost Engineering Consulting Service.

## 1. The Funnel Mathematics

**Baseline Assumptions (Monthly):**
- **Traffic**: 10,000 unique visitors
- **Audit Completion Rate**: 15% (1,500 audits generated)
- **High-Savings Qualification Rate**: 30% of audits show >$500/mo in savings (450 qualified leads)
- **Consultation Booking Rate**: 10% of qualified leads click the CTA and book a call (45 booked calls)
- **Close Rate**: 20% of calls convert to paying clients (9 new clients/mo)

## 2. Customer Acquisition Cost (CAC)
Because the primary GTM motion is organic (Product Hunt, Twitter teardowns, Reddit), our blended CAC is exceptionally low.

- **Organic Content Cost**: ~$2,000/mo (Founder time / Fractional Content Writer)
- **Paid Ads (Retargeting only)**: $500/mo
- **Total Marketing Spend**: $2,500/mo
- **CAC**: $2,500 / 9 clients = **$277 per acquired client**

## 3. Lifetime Value (LTV) & Revenue Model
We do not charge a SaaS fee. We charge a **performance fee** based on the savings we successfully implement, plus a retainer for ongoing API optimization.

**Pricing Structure:**
1. **The Audit Implementation Fee**: 20% of first-year realized savings.
   - *Average Client Savings*: $1,200/mo ($14,400/yr)
   - *Implementation Fee*: **$2,880** (One-time)
2. **Ongoing API Optimization Retainer**: **$500/mo** (For large clients needing continuous prompt/token optimization).
   - Assume 30% of clients take the retainer. Average lifespan = 12 months.
   - *Retainer LTV*: $6,000 * 30% = **$1,800**

**Blended LTV**: $2,880 + $1,800 = **$4,680**

## 4. Profitability Metrics
- **LTV:CAC Ratio**: $4,680 / $277 = **16.8x** (Extremely healthy, indicating we should heavily scale paid acquisition once organic is proven).
- **Payback Period**: Immediate. The implementation fee is collected upfront or in month 1, meaning we recover the $277 CAC instantly.

## 5. ARR Trajectory (Year 1)
If we maintain 9 new clients per month:
- **Total Clients Year 1**: 108
- **One-time Revenue**: 108 * $2,880 = $311,040
- **Retainer ARR**: ~32 clients * $500/mo * 12 = $192,000
- **Total Gross Revenue (Year 1)**: **$503,040**

*Conclusion*: The free audit tool serves as an incredibly capital-efficient wedge to build a highly profitable, half-million-dollar bootstrap consulting business in Year 1, which can later be productized into a B2B SaaS platform.
